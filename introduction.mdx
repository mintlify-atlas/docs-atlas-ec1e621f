tModLoader (TML) is an open-source, community-driven modification and expansion of Terraria that makes it possible to create and play mods. TML extends Terraria with a rich C# API, giving modders access to hooks, custom content types, and Steam Workshop integration — all without modifying the base game's source.

## How tModLoader works

tModLoader patches the Terraria binary at runtime, injecting its modding layer without distributing Terraria's proprietary source code. When you install TML via Steam, it appears alongside your Terraria installation and manages loading, unloading, and compatibility checking for all enabled mods.

<Note>
  tModLoader requires a valid copy of Terraria on Steam. It is available as a free DLC on the [Steam store](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1281930/tModLoader/).
</Note>
